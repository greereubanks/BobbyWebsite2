The St. Louis Arch

Basic Facts

  • Inside the arch you can ride a capsal tram that takes you to the top.
  • The arch is the tallest human made monument in the United States.
  • The arch commemorates Americas westward expansion.
  • The structure is also called the Gateway Arch.

Interesting Facts

  • Because of extreme weather the arch was designed to sway!
  • On the last day of building the arch, the heat caused the steel to expand causing a peice to not fit! They ended up spraying the structure with cold water!
  • A local rule states that the arch has to be the tallest structure in the area!
  • The arch has used the most stainless steal in any project ever!
  • The arch is just as wide as it is tall(630 feet) causing an optical illusion!
